Our six-step delivery

  1. Initial contact — goals, constraints, one-page proposal.

  3. Analysis & design — ADRs, architecture notes, estimates.
  4. Build & transition — sprints, weekly demos, tests, docs.
  5. Operate & support — monitoring, incidents, small fixes.
  6. Review & improve — KPI + cost review, next-step proposals.
